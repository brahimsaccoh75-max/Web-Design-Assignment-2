CONTACT & FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

HOW DO I BUY TICKETS?

National team tickets are sold online via the SLFA portal and at designated bank branches in Freetown one week prior to kickoff.

AGENCY MEMBERSHIP

Regional FAs and clubs can apply for membership through the General Secretary's office at the Secretariat.

MEDIA ACCREDITATION

Journalists must apply for season accreditation via the communications department.
